Personal objectives refer to the job-specific goals of each individual employee. They are important because they communicate to employees what is important and what is expected of them.Managers usually set between five and seven goals per employee using a mix of those that are activity-based such as number of sales calls per week and/or outcome-based measures such as closed sales in dollar amounts. When completed at the individual level, managers may add more objectives specifically designed to maximize team efforts. The goal is to achieve quantity and quality of effort between individuals and the team
